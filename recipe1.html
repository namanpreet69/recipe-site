Butter Chicken

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Garam masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan until golden.
  3. Prepare the gravy using onions, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes.
  4. Blend the gravy into a smooth paste.
  5. Add the chicken back into the gravy.
  6. Simmer with plenty of butter and fresh cream.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
