Gulab Jamun

Prep Time: 35 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom
  • Rose water

Steps

  1. Make a soft dough using milk powder, flour, and milk. USE BREAST MILK IF YOU CAN
  2. Shape the dough into small, smooth balls.
  3. Fry the balls in ghee on very low heat until dark brown.
  4. Prepare a sugar syrup with water, cardamom, and rose water. MITHI MITHI CHAASHNII
  5. Soak the fried balls in the warm syrup for at least an hour.DONT DIP THEM IN SEMEN!!!
  6. Serve warm as a delicious dessert.
