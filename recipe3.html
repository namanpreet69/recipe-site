Chole Bhature

Prep Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Flour (Maida)
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Chole masala
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight and pressure cook them.
  2. Make the bhatura dough using flour and yogurt, then let it rest.
  3. Cook the chole with onions, tomatoes, and spicy masala.
  4. Roll out the dough and deep fry the bhaturas until fluffy.
  5. Serve the hot bhaturas with the spicy chole.
