Biryani

Prep Time: 60 minutes

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani masala
  • Saffron
  • Mint leaves
  • Ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices.
  3. Fry the onions until they are crispy and brown.
  4. Layer the parboiled rice and marinated chicken in a heavy pot.
  5. Cook on "dum" (slow steam) for the flavors to mix.
  6. Garnish with saffron and mint, then serve.
