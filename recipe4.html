Paneer Tikka

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Yogurt
  • Capsicum
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Tikka masala
  • Lemon juice
  • Oil ps dont use engine oil for health safety, fda blah blah reasons
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer and vegetables into large cubes.
  2. Marinate them with spices, lemon juice, and yogurt.
  3. Skewer the paneer cubes along with the vegetables.
  4. Grill in an oven or cook on a flat pan until charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.
